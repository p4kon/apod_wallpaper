# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

APOD Wallpaper is designed to keep app data local to the user's device.

## Data stored locally

APOD Wallpaper may store the following data on your device:

- app settings, such as wallpaper style, auto-check preference, startup preference, and selected image folder
- cached APOD metadata, such as image/video availability by date
- downloaded APOD images
- logs used for troubleshooting
- an optional NASA API key, if you choose to enter one

The NASA API key is stored locally and is used only for NASA APOD requests.

## Network access

APOD Wallpaper connects to NASA APOD services to retrieve APOD metadata, HTML pages, images, and related content.

The app may open APOD pages in your default web browser when you choose to open a NASA page.

## Data sharing

APOD Wallpaper does not include analytics, advertising, telemetry, or in-app purchases.

The app does not sell personal data.

The app does not intentionally send your settings, downloaded images, or NASA API key to the developer.

Requests sent to NASA services are governed by NASA's own terms, privacy practices, rate limits, and availability.

## Local files and wallpaper

If enabled by you, APOD Wallpaper can:

- download images to the selected local folder
- change your desktop wallpaper
- start with Windows
- keep running in the system tray
